# Business Recommendations

Based on the SQL analysis, the following strategic recommendations are proposed:

## Regional Performance

Investigate pricing strategies and operational costs in lower-performing regions while replicating successful practices from high-performing regions.

## Product Performance

Increase investment in high-profit product categories and review pricing strategies for low-margin products.

## Customer Segmentation

Reward VIP customers while developing engagement campaigns for Regular and Low Value customers.

## Discount Strategy

Review discount policies and introduce approval thresholds for large discounts to protect profitability.

## Sales Monitoring

Track Month-over-Month and Year-over-Year growth to identify changes in business performance.

## Inventory Planning

Align inventory levels and marketing campaigns with seasonal demand patterns.
